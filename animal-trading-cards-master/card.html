Sloth

Three-toed sloths can turn their heads almost 360 degrees.

  • Scientific Name: Folivora
  • Average Length: 2 to 2.5 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: Central and South America

Sloths—the sluggish tree-dwellers of Central and South America—spend their lives in the tropical rain forests. There are two different types of sloths, two-toed and three-toed, and six species.
